About me

I'm a mexican Full-Stack Developer Developer with two years experience. I have a BFA in Graphic Design with a focus on Typography and Editorial Design and I'm currently pursuing a technical degree in Software Engineering and plan on continuing my education with a Masters in Design Thinking.

A shift in my professional activity from Editorial design to Web Development, starting off as a UI/UX designer, got me gradually more involved in programming, ultimately falling in love with the Django Framework for web app development.

Hired full-time for the past two years, I was offered in-house training as a Full-Stack Developer and support for continuing my education as a Software Engineer in hopes of hybridizing my Designing career with an IT approach.

I'm passionate about Information Technologies and Design, Semiotics and Data Science.
